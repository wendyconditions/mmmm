Memorial Service and Additional Information

We will be holding a small memorial service for our father at one of his favorite locations in Santa Barbara-- the Voskly Prayer Chapel Westmont College. This was the location of our parents’ wedding and frequent family trips. Due to the limited capacity of the venue, we are limiting the service to close family and friends. We also believe our father would not have wanted anything large and we would prefer something more intimate as well.

We invite everyone to honor their memories of our father by:

  1. Sending flowers in his honor (his favorites were easter lilies and camelias) to the address below. Flowers may be delivered February 7th.
  2. Making a donation to the Child Fund on his behalf, one of our father’s favorite charities.

Thank you again for your support and eagerness to pay respects to our father and family. We appreciate everyone’s love for him.

We invite those who are unable to attend to send flowers the day prior to 4576 Aragon Dr., Capinteria, CA 93013 or consider making a donation to Child Fund, one of our father’s favorite charities.
