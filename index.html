Memorial Service and Additional Information

W e will be holding a small memorial service for our father at one of his favorite locations in Santa Barbara-- the Voskuyl Prayer Chapel at Westmont College. This was the location of our parents’ wedding and frequent family trips. Due to the limited capacity of the venue, we are only able to include close family and friends.

We invite everyone to honor their memories of our father by:

  1. Sending flowers for his service (his favorites were Easter Lilies and Camelias) to the address below, or
  2. Making a donation to the Child Fund on his behalf, one of our father’s favorite charities.

For those wishing to send flowers, flowers can be delivered to 4576 Aragon Dr., Carpinteria, CA 93013 on February 7th.

Thank you for your support and kind words during this time. We know our father was special to many and we are grateful for all who appreciated his generosity, humor, and wisdom.

Share your memories of Mark:

Our father was a storyteller, or a teller of the same stories over and over again. Help us remember his stories by writing down your favorite memories of Mark. These will be collated and shared with close family.

Memories will be sent to Celene Mulholland and be shared with family.
